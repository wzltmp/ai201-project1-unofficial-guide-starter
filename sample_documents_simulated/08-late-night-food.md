# #food — "it's 11pm and I'm starving, what's open?"

**nightowl_nick:** ok pinning the real answer to this because it gets asked every night.
after ~8–9pm most halls are closed. your options ranked:

**On campus, latest hot food:**
- **North Quad Dining** is the latest-closing hall, open till **midnight** on weeknights.
  if it's before midnight and you want an actual hot meal, go here. the stir-fry wok closes
  ~11:30 though so don't cut it too close.
- **The Marketplace grab-and-go fridge + convenience corner** stays open after the hot
  stations close — pre-made sandwiches, wraps, snacks, microwaveable stuff. not hot-cooked
  but open late and takes dining dollars.

**On campus, 24/7-ish:**
- the **convenience store in the student union** (basically a campus mini-mart) is open
  latest of anything on campus, like 1am most nights. ramen, frozen meals, snacks, energy
  drinks. dining dollars work here.

**Off campus, when everything else is dead:**
- **Moonlight Diner** — the 24-hour diner two blocks off the east gate. classic 2am
  pancakes spot. cash/card, no dining dollars obviously.
- **Slice Night Pizza** — open till 3am thurs–sat, till 1am weekdays. delivers to the dorms.
- the food trucks on College Ave hang around till ~1am on weekends (the taco one is goated).

**nightowl_nick:** tldr: before midnight → North Quad. after midnight → union convenience
store on campus, or Moonlight Diner / Slice off campus.
