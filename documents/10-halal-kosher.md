Summary of U-M Dining's religious-observance options.

South Quad houses the only kosher kitchen on campus and also has a halal station. Bursley
features a Halal station serving composed halal entrées. Across all dining halls, menu items
are flagged with halal and kosher icons so students can identify them quickly, and
registered dietitians are available to help students plan around religious dietary needs.
