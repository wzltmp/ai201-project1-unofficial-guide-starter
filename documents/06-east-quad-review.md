Excerpt from The Michigan Daily's "Campus Culture reviews: Dining hall edition, Part 2."

East Quad is much smaller than South Quad — "This dining hall was ¼ the size of South Quad,
and not knowing what station had what food was both fun and annoying." The payoff is
intimacy and service: "There was an intimacy that came with being at a smaller dining hall —
I noticed the extra care put into everything." East Quad is known for sustainably-minded
options and its Residential College atmosphere, but vegetarian variety is limited and the
seitan was a disappointment.
