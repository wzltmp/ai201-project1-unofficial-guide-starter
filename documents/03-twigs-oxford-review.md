Excerpt from The Michigan Daily's "Campus Culture reviews: Dining hall edition."

Twigs, tucked away at Oxford Residence Hall in a woodland setting, is small and calm, with
comfortable couches and minimal lines. It runs a single rotating "Signature Maize" station
that offers a vegan option, a meat option, and a carb option every day, plus a stocked
salad and sandwich bar with fresh toppings and a yogurt bar with fruit. Desserts are
elevated — whoopie pies with tahini filling and s'mores bars. A dining staff member, Susan,
described the Oxford residents as "a close family."
