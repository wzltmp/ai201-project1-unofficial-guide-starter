Excerpt summarizing The Michigan Daily's reporting on U-M meal plans and rates.

U-M offers tiered residential meal plans. The 125-Block plan includes about 28 dining-hall
entrances and $55.56 in Blue Bucks each month. The Unlimited Basic plan costs roughly $575
per month across the nine fall and winter months — about $19 per day — which is reasonable
for unlimited access if you eat in the dining halls daily, but students who go home on
weekends or skip meals end up losing money on it. If you instead pay per visit, single
meals run about $9.50 for breakfast and $15.25 for lunch or dinner. Blue Bucks (and Dining
Dollars) can be spent at the dining halls and at campus cafés and markets.
