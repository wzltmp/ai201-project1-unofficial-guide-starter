Summary of U-M Dining's vegan/vegetarian options (M|Dining nutrition info and Michigan
Daily reviews).

Every dish on the menu is tagged with icons — including vegan and vegetarian — and the
"MyNutrition" site lets students filter for vegan or vegetarian items. Bursley runs a
dedicated vegan and vegetarian station called "24 Carrots." Twigs' rotating "Signature
Maize" station always includes a vegan option each day. At South Quad, vegetarians can eat
well but often have to combine sides creatively. East Quad offers vegetarian options but
with limited variety, and one reviewer panned its seitan.
