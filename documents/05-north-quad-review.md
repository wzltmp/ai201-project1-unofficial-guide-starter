Excerpt from The Michigan Daily's "Campus Culture reviews: Dining hall edition, Part 2."

North Quad is compact and convenient for a quick meal, but limited: "Compared to the other
dining halls, North Quad can feel small and limited in variety." Breakfast was rated better
than lunch. The reviewer praised the sustainability effort: "The waste area boasted a
refreshing change; every item I'd grabbed since walking in, from trays to utensils, was
compostable."
