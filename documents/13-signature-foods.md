Excerpt from The Michigan Daily's dining hall reviews, highlighting standout items.

A few signature items define U-M dining, and dessert is consistently where it shines
brightest. The most famous is the Mojo cookie at Mosher-Jordan — "a chocolate-chip
masterpiece undercooked to the perfect degree of gooeyness." South Quad's self-serve ice
cream machine and fried pita chips are favorites, and you can build your own sundae there.
Twigs is known for elevated desserts like whoopie pies with tahini filling and s'mores bars.
