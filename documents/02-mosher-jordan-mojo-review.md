Excerpt from The Michigan Daily's "Campus Culture reviews: Dining hall edition."

Mosher-Jordan, known as "Mojo," is famous for its desserts — "Mojo's dessert always reigns
supreme." The signature draw is the Mojo cookie, "a chocolate-chip masterpiece undercooked
to the perfect degree of gooeyness," though it is not always in stock. Other desserts
include whoopie pies with tahini filling and cereal s'mores bars. The savory food is more
uneven; one lunch featured plain spaghetti in a sweet jarred sauce. The hall has also seen
small changes, like plastic silverware replacing older versions.
